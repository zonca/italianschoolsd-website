Zelle is our **preferred payment method** because it allows 100% of your tuition to support our educational programs without any credit card processing fees.

## Why Choose Zelle?

*   **No Fees**: Avoid the 3.6% processing fee associated with credit card payments.
*   **Integrated**: Zelle is already built into over 1,000 banking apps (like Chase, Bank of America, and Wells Fargo). It is **not** a separate third-party app like Venmo or PayPal—it's part of the banking system you already use.
*   **Fast & Secure**: Your payment is sent directly from your bank to ours, typically in minutes.

## How to Pay

1.  **Open your banking app** (or sign in to your bank's website).
2.  Navigate to the **Send Money with Zelle®** section.
3.  Add the Italian School as a recipient:
    *   **Email**: **pay@italianschoolsd.com**
    *   **Name**: **Italian school of San Diego LLC**
4.  Enter the amount.
5.  **Important**: In the "Memo" or "Note" field, please include:
    *   The **student's name**.
    *   The **class** or **invoice number**.
6.  Confirm and send!

## FAQ

**Is Zelle like Venmo?**
No. While Venmo is a third-party wallet, Zelle is a service provided by the banks themselves. It is embedded directly in your existing banking app, making it more secure and easier to track.

**Can I use a business account?**
Yes, most business banking accounts also support Zelle for small business payments.

**I don't see Zelle in my app.**
Check the [Zelle website](https://www.zellepay.com/get-started) to see if your bank is a partner. Most major US banks are included.
