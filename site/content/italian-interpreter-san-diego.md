The Italian School of San Diego provides professional Italian-English and English-Italian interpreting services. Whether you are hosting an international business delegation, organizing a conference, or planning a private tour, our native Italian interpreters ensure clear, accurate, and culturally nuanced communication.

---

## Our Interpreting Services {#services}

We support a wide range of settings, including:

- **Business Meetings & Negotiations:** Facilitate smooth discussions, corporate negotiations, and partner meetings with professional consecutive interpreting.
- **Conferences & Seminars:** Support speakers or attendees at business events, academic conferences, and industry summits.
- **Tours & Travel Delegation:** Accompany Italian-speaking visitors, executives, or groups on guided tours, company site visits, and social itineraries in San Diego and California.
- **Private & Cultural Events:** Provide interpreting for family milestones, community events, and private gatherings.

*Please note: We specialize in in-person interpreting services within San Diego County and surrounding areas in Southern California. For events elsewhere in California, travel arrangements can be discussed.*

## Why Choose The Italian School? {#why-us}

- **Native Italian Speakers:** All our interpreters are mother-tongue Italian speakers with deep understanding of regional dialects, cultural context, and formal/informal nuances.
- **Professional Conduct:** We maintain strict confidentiality and follow professional code of conduct standards for all assignments.
- **Cultural Liaison:** Beyond converting words, our interpreters act as cultural bridges, helping you build stronger relationships with your Italian counterparts.
