Italian On Demand is our new private lesson option for students who want more flexibility than a standard weekly class. You choose an open slot, book at least **12 hours in advance**, and meet online via **Zoom** for a **one-hour private lesson**.

This service is designed for:

* complete beginners who want to start with a teacher one-on-one
* students already taking Italian who want extra speaking practice
* intermediate and advanced learners who want a class tailored to their goals
* adults preparing for travel, family communication, citizenship, school, or professional use

## How it works

1. View the available lesson times on our booking form.
2. Choose the slot that works best for you.
3. Pay online.
4. Receive your calendar invite and Zoom link automatically.

Each lesson is:

* **1 hour**
* **$80**
* **online via Zoom**
* **private**
* **paid one class at a time**
* **no long-term commitment**
* **not refundable**

There is **no commitment** to a package, semester, or recurring schedule. You do **not** need to sign up for multiple lessons in advance. You simply book and pay for **one class at a time** whenever you want a lesson.

This makes Italian On Demand a good fit if you want to:

* try a private lesson before committing to a regular class
* book only occasionally when your schedule allows
* add one extra lesson from time to time without changing your standard routine

Once a lesson is booked, it is **not refundable**.

## Who it is for

We can support learners from **complete beginner** through **intermediate** and **advanced** levels.

Lessons can focus on:

* conversation
* pronunciation
* grammar review
* listening comprehension
* travel Italian
* school support
* reading and writing
* confidence building for real-life communication

Our teaching staff for this program is **native Italian**, and lessons are adapted to the student's level, pace, and objectives.
