Relocating to Italy as a remote worker requires a multi-disciplinary approach. Our team collaborates on a single timeline, ensuring your visa application, tax setup, registered lease, and travel planning are fully aligned.

## **Coordinator** – Italian school of San Diego Staff
**Project Management & Timeline Tracking**

A coordinator from the Italian School of San Diego serves as your primary contact. The coordinator:
*   Aligns all specialists (legal, tax, housing, travel) so there are no scheduling or document conflicts.
*   Maintains your master project checklist.
*   Provides access to your private dashboard to track relocation milestones in real-time.

## **Immigration & Citizenship Law** – External Counsel
**Visa Pre-screening, Consular Applications & Residency Compliance**

Our external legal counsel is an Italian-American attorney with law degrees from both the U.S. and Italy. She provides:
*   Eligibility screening to verify "highly qualified" credentials.
*   Assistance formatting employment contracts and drafting employer declarations.
*   Guidance on Prenot@mi bookings and fingerprinting requirements.
*   Coordination for the post-arrival *Permesso di Soggiorno*.
*   *Booking link:* [Book a consultation here](https://calendly.com/r-gentilipurcell)

## **Luigi Parente, CPA (US & Italy)** – Cross‑Border Taxation
**Partita IVA Setup, Regime Forfettario & Corporate Payroll Compliance**

Luigi is a dual-qualified Certified Public Accountant in both the U.S. (New York) and Italy, with over a decade of cross-border tax experience. For remote professionals, Luigi handles:
*   Evaluation of tax residency implications (the 183-day rule).
*   Setup of *Partita IVA* under the 5% flat-tax *Regime Forfettario* for freelancers.
*   Coordination of payroll compliance and Employer of Record (EOR) options for corporate employees.
*   Treaty application (FEIE, FTC) to eliminate double taxation.

## **Claudia Cencelli** – Real Estate Strategy
**Consulate-Compliant Leases & Property Search**

Claudia is a licensed Realtor specializing in assisting Americans relocating to Italy. Born in Rome and practicing real estate in Southern California for 20 years, Claudia bridges expectations:
*   Finding landlords willing to sign 12-month residential contracts remotely.
*   Ensuring the lease is officially registered with the *Agenzia delle Entrate*.
*   Securing the mandatory registration receipt required for your visa interview.

## **Manuela Broccolini** – Travel & Relocation Planning
**Scouting Trips & Settlement Logistics**

With over 12 years of experience in high-end Italian travel planning, Manuela designs custom trips that allow you to "try on" your new life:
*   Coordinating scouting trips to evaluate internet speeds and co-working spaces.
*   Managing local logistics for utilities activation, SIM cards, and pet travel.
