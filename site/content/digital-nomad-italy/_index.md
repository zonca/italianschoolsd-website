Moving to Italy as a digital nomad or remote worker involves navigating unique legal, tax, and logistical challenges. Our team provides professional guidance to streamline this process, ensuring your transition is clear, compliant, and efficient.

We replace the fragmentation of separate advisors with a fully coordinated approach, managing your visa, tax setup, and housing through a single point of contact.

## Core Services for Nomads

We provide comprehensive support across five critical pillars of relocation:

- **Visa & Residency:** Direct assistance with the Digital Nomad and Remote Worker Visa application, including eligibility screening, document review, and consular preparation.
- **Tax Planning & Compliance:** Optimizing your U.S. and Italian tax structure, including setup of the **Regime Forfettario (5% flat tax)** for freelancers or compliance coordination for W-2 remote employees.
- **Housing & Leases:** Finding and securing a long-term rental contract (*Contratto di Locazione*) and completing the mandatory registration with the *Agenzia delle Entrate*.
- **Logistics & Setup:** Administrative support for obtaining your *Codice Fiscale*, applying for your *Permesso di Soggiorno* within 8 days, and setting up local bank accounts.
- **Integration:** Structured scouting trips, language learning, and community settling support.
