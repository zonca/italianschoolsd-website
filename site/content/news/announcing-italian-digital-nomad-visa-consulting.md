The Italian School of San Diego is expanding its relocation services to include dedicated consulting for Italy's newly launched **Digital Nomad Visa**. 

Designed for remote workers, freelance professionals, and self-employed individuals in California, this service provides step-by-step guidance on consular visa requirements, *Codice Fiscale* acquisition, housing logistics, and the residency permit (*Permesso di Soggiorno*) application in Italy.

For more details on how we can help you move your remote work to Italy, please visit our new [Italian Digital Nomad Visa]({{< ref "italian-digital-nomad-visa-san-diego.md" >}}) page.
