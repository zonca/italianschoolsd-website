We have extended the pre-enrollment deadline for our **2026-2027 Italian classes for kids and teens** to **July 27, 2026**.

If you haven't submitted your pre-enrollment yet, please do so by **July 27** to secure your spot for the upcoming academic year.

## Enrollment & Payment Procedure

1. **Submit Pre-Enrollment Form:** Pre-enroll online for [Kids Classes (TK-6th Grade)](/enroll) or [Accredited Teen Classes](/enroll-accredited) by **July 27, 2026**. Pre-enrollment is non-binding and requires no payment up front.
2. **Confirmation & Invoice:** We will confirm class placements and send an invoice with tuition details to your email.
3. **Finalize Enrollment:** To lock in your spot, send payment by **August 3, 2026** (or within **1 week** after receiving the payment confirmation email).
