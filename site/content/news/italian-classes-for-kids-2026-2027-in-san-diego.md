Pre-enrollment is open for our **2026-2027 Italian classes for kids** in San Diego.

We offer small-group classes for TK-6th grade students at our Kearny Mesa location, with programs for Italian heritage speakers, beginners, and homeschool families.

## 2026-2027 Schedule {#schedule}

* **Italian Heritage classes:** Thursdays, **4:15-6:15pm**, from **August 20, 2026** to **May 13, 2027**. Backup date: **May 20, 2027**. No class on **December 17, 2026**.
* **Italian as a Foreign Language classes:** Wednesdays, **4-6pm**, from **August 19, 2026** to **May 12, 2027**. Backup date: **May 19, 2027**.
* **Homeschool morning program:** Wednesdays, **10am-12pm**, organized as two 16-class sessions: **August 26, 2026-January 13, 2027** and **January 20-May 12, 2027**.

Classes have 5-10 students and are grouped by age and level.

## Pre-Enrollment {#pre-enrollment}

Please pre-enroll by **July 31, 2026** to secure a spot. Pre-enrollment is not binding; we will confirm the proposed class and send an invoice to finalize enrollment.
