We are excited to announce that the Italian School of San Diego is expanding its services to include professional Italian-English and English-Italian interpreting. 

Our team of native Italian speakers is available for in-person interpreting assignments across San Diego County and Southern California. We support a variety of settings, including:

- **Business & Corporate:** Meetings, presentations, and contract negotiations.
- **Tours & Travel:** Accompaniment for Italian-speaking individuals, delegations, or groups.
- **Conferences & Events:** Support for speakers and attendees at summits or conventions.

By providing clear, accurate, and culturally nuanced communication, we help ensure your business meetings, tours, or events run smoothly. 

For more details on our services, please visit our new [Italian Interpreter Services]({{< ref "italian-interpreter-san-diego.md" >}}) page. For document translation requests (such as legal certificates or transcripts), please refer to our [Italian Translation Services]({{< ref "translate/_index.md" >}}) page.

If you have an upcoming event or tour and would like to request an interpreter, please [contact us](/contact) to discuss your requirements and get a quote.
