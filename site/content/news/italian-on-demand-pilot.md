description: We are launching Italian On Demand, a new pilot program for one-hour private Italian lessons booked at least 12 hours in advance.
---

This pilot is designed for students who want a flexible private lesson option without committing to a regular weekly schedule. Lessons are one hour, taught online via Zoom, and booked one class at a time rather than as a package or semester commitment. This pilot is currently intended for students who can book at least 12 hours in advance.
