## Retire to Italy

Dreaming of moving to Italy? We now offer expert coordination to help you navigate the complex legal, financial, and logistical challenges of relocation. Our team of external experts works together to provide a streamlined experience, covering:

- **Visa & Residency:** Strategic analysis and application management.
- **Tax Planning:** Coordination of U.S. and Italian tax strategies, including information on the **7% flat tax rate** for pensioners.
- **Real Estate:** Support for renting or buying property.
- **Logistics & Integration:** Health service registration, banking, and settling in.

We provide a single point of contact to manage your entire transition.
