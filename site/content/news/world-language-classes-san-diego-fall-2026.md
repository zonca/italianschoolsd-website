Our new World Languages program offers three in-person beginner classes for adults on Tuesday evenings in Kearny Mesa. Choose Spanish, German, or English for Spanish speakers.

All three classes meet **Tuesdays from 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM**, September 15-December 8, 2026. There is no class November 24. Each course includes 12 classes and costs $432 in full or four monthly payments of $118.80.

Each class must reach five paid students by noon on September 8. If the school cancels a class because it does not reach the minimum enrollment, enrolled students will receive a full refund.

## Beginner Spanish for Adults {#spanish}

Build practical Spanish skills through conversation, listening, vocabulary, reading, writing, and foundational grammar. This class is for adults starting Spanish from the beginning.

- **Schedule:** Tuesdays, 6:00 PM-7:30 PM
- **Dates:** September 15-December 8, 2026
- **No class:** November 24
- **Location:** [4550 Kearny Villa Rd, Suite 202, San Diego](/location/)
- **Tuition:** $432 in full or four monthly payments of $118.80
- **Textbook:** Details will be provided before the first class

### Policies and Discounts {#policies}

- **Family discount:** For full-payment enrollment in the same class, the first student pays full tuition and each additional student from the same family receives 10% off automatically.
- **Monthly plan:** Four payments of $118.80 total $475.20 and include a 10% administrative fee. The plan is for one student per checkout and commits the student to all four payments.
- **School cancellation:** If a class does not reach five paid students by September 8, the school will cancel that class and issue a full refund.
- **Student cancellations:** Full refund if canceled at least 15 days before the first class; 50% refund after that deadline but before the second class; no refunds after the second class begins.

Questions? [Contact us](/contact/) and we will be happy to help.
