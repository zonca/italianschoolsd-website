This fall, our new world languages program offers three in-person beginner classes for adults on Tuesday evenings in Kearny Mesa. Choose beginner Spanish, beginner German, or English for Spanish-speaking adults.

All three classes meet **Tuesdays from 6:00 PM to 7:30 PM**, September 15-December 8, 2026, with no class on November 24. Each course includes twelve 90-minute classes. Tuition is $432 in full or four monthly payments of $118.80.

Each class requires at least five paid enrollments by noon on September 8. If a class does not reach the minimum, the school will cancel that class and issue full refunds.

## Beginner Spanish for adults {#spanish}

Build a practical foundation in Spanish through conversation, listening, vocabulary, reading, writing, and grammar. This class is designed for adults with little or no prior knowledge of Spanish.

- **Schedule:** Tuesdays, 6:00 PM-7:30 PM
- **Dates:** September 15-December 8, 2026
- **No class:** November 24
- **Location:** [4550 Kearny Villa Rd, Suite 202, San Diego](/location/)
- **Tuition:** $432 in full or four monthly payments of $118.80
- **Textbook:** Details will be provided before the first class

### Policies and discounts {#policies}

- **Family discount:** For full-payment enrollment in the same class, the first student pays full tuition and each additional student from the same family receives 10% off automatically.
- **Monthly plan:** The four-payment plan costs $475.20 total (four payments of $118.80), including a 10% administrative fee. It covers one student per checkout and requires all four payments.
- **School cancellation:** If a class does not reach five paid students by September 8, the school will cancel that class and issue a full refund.
- **Student cancellations:** Full refund if canceled at least 15 days before the first class; 50% refund after that deadline but before the second class; no refunds after the second class begins.

Questions? [Contact us](/contact/) and we will be happy to help.
